                  About us

                  Rahat Bakery is a cozy spot in the heart of Da Nang, where traditions and the warmth of homemade bread come together We bake bread in a tandoor and specialize in authentic Uzbek and Tatar cuisine. Our team is made up of people from different cultures, all united under the roof of our bakery to create something truly special for you

                  Our symbol is inspired by the shape of a tandoor flatbread: it’s just as warm and radiant. Just like our name, as "Rahat" means peace, warm and tranquility
